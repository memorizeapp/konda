Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Konda is designed to make staying in touch simple and thoughtful. Privacy is extremely important to us and we aim to only collect the information needed to provide app functionality. We do not run ads or sell your data to any third parties.

Data we collect and store

  • Account & identifiers: We collect account information such as username, nickname and device tokens to be able to send and deliver nudges.
  • Contacts: Approved connections between accounts (who you’ve added/accepted).
  • Nudges: The nudge type you send/receive and timestamps (sender, recipient, sent time).

Where data is stored

  • Server: Account IDs, usernames/nicknames, contacts, nudge history (type + sender/recipient/time), device tokens are stored securely on a virtual private server hosted by Linode.
  • iCloud: Your nudge history, favorites, groups, pending sends; scoped to your personal Apple ID’s private iCloud, if you have iCloud enabled for the app.
  • On-device: Local caches and pending items are temporarily stored on device for performance/offline use and general functionality of the app.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you (Sign in with Apple).
  • Deliver nudges and notifications (push to phone/watch).
  • Maintain contacts, streaks and inbox history.
  • Sync phone ↔ watch and iCloud backups.

We only use the data we collect to provide the service. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Push delivery goes through Apple Push Notification service.
  • iCloud storage is via your private CloudKit database.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, such as:

  • Transport over HTTPS.
  • Limited access controls for our development team.

Retention & deletion

  • Data persists while your account is active.
  • If you choose to delete your account, we automatically delete all associated data from our servers. You can choose to delete any data stored on your personal iCloud account through your iCloud settings.

Children

Konda is not intended for children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any of your data stored in our systems
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

Changes to Privacy Policy

Users will be notified of any changes to this privacy policy with an in-app notification. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
